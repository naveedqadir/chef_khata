About Chef Khata

A free, voice-first AI cooking assistant that lets you have natural conversations about recipes, cooking techniques, and ingredient substitutions ‚Äî hands-free in your kitchen.

Why Chef Khata?

Cooking should be enjoyable, not stressful. But scrolling through recipe pages with flour-covered hands or pausing a video every 10 seconds isn't fun. Chef Khata solves this with a hands-free voice agent that understands context, remembers what you said, and responds with a warm, human-like voice.

Just tap the orb (or press spacebar) and ask anything ‚Äî "How do I poach an egg?", "What can I substitute for buttermilk?", or "Give me a quick dinner idea with chicken and broccoli." Chef Khata responds conversationally, keeping answers short and actionable.

You can even interrupt mid-sentence by simply talking ‚Äî the chef stops, listens to your follow-up, and responds with full context of your conversation. It feels like chatting with a real person in the kitchen.
